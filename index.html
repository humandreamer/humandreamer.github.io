HumanDreamer: Animating Your Single Image into Controllable Videos

GigaAI 

Abstract

In this work, we propose HumanDreamer, an approach that can animate a given single human image into controlled videos. HumanDreamer is capable of handling various types of human images, including selfies, portraits, multiple bodies images, in-the-wild images, generated images, cartoon characters, traditional Chinese costume images, and human sculptures. In terms of generating sequences, HumanDreamer can generate various complex action videos based on pose templates. Thanks to comprehensive learning of the dynamic variations in scenes during the training process, the background in the generated videos also undergoes reasonable changes according to the content. Compared to the latest work in human animation, Animate Anyone, HumanDreamer exhibits more emerging capabilities in multiple bodies movement, facial expressions, clothing variations, and background movements. HumanDreamer has numerous potential applications, such as personalized dance video generation, fashion model showcasing, art creation, and virtual digital humans.
